Meet Our Team

Ashleigh Brown

Ashleigh is the Founder of this baking school. She was trained in well-knowned Le Cordon Bleu London, UK. She has passions for teaching and ambition of making baking an easy learned skills for everyone.

Gordon Hines

Gordon was trained in well-knowned Le Cordon Bleu Paris, France. He owned and ran a Patisserie shop in Paris for 5 years before returning home to Dublin to be with his parents.

Our Kitchen

Our kitchen was originally a cottage which Ashleigh owned since 1996. She has converted the cottage to a well-equipped studio kitchen for teaching. It can accommodate students up to group of ten.
